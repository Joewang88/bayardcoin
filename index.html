About Me

Joe Wang

Specialist in Chinese and World Coins

I have been collecting and dealing with coins since 2012. I specialize in Chinese coins but have coins from many other countries as well. I also handle a broad range of numismatic literature related with world coins. I attend shows and auctions worldwide purchasing those rare coins.

If you have coins for sale, please give me a call at 4123308868, or email me at info@bayardcoin.com. All purchases and sales are private to protect your confidentiality. I will never share your information with others.
